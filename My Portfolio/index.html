01 — About

Engineer by study,
builder by passion

I'm Abidul Hasan Amin, a CSE student at SBPGC, Bangladesh. I build things for the web — from clean frontend interfaces to full project structures. I've worked as a Graphic Designer at Tasfiya Telecom, handled all banner design for my college, built the college website's frontend, and assisted seniors on multiple development projects.

Beyond tech, I'm a content creator, debater, department representative, and founder of Amin's Malaysian Board/Craft. I mentor students in HTML and serve as admin/moderator across multiple CSE communities.

I compete in programming contests, enjoy building real things, and I'm always looking for the next problem worth solving.
